Step Button

A 44px round tap target for incrementing or decrementing a value. Paired with a numeric to form a + / − group for water, scoops, reps, etc.

Anatomy

A 44×44 raised pill button with an 18px Phosphor glyph centered. Border is hairline; background shifts down on press.

  • Size 44 × 44, meets the 44pt minimum tap target.
  • Surface --c-raised bg, --c-hair border, --c-ink icon.
  • Press bg shifts to --c-hair via :active, --m-fast.
  • Disabled --c-faint icon, opacity 0.6.

Variants

Minus and plus. The glyph is the only difference; the class is identical.
