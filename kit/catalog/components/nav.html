FORGE · Kit Components

Nav

The floating frosted bottom nav. Four tabs, capsule-shaped, fixed to the safe area. The active tab is amber; inactive tabs are faint.

Anatomy

A pill-shaped bar that floats above content. Backdrop-filter blur lets the screen show through. Each tab is a 44 × 44 round button — meets minimum tap target.

Anatomy
  • Surface 72% black, 20px blur backdrop-filter, hairline border.
  • Shape --r-pill radius · --sh-nav shadow.
  • Position fixed, bottom-anchored, max-width 374px, centered, respects safe-area-inset-bottom.
  • Buttons 44 × 44 each, evenly spaced, 20px icon.
  • Active aria-current="page" OR .is-active — flips icon to --c-amber.

Active tab states

Each tab shown as the active one in turn. Active is the only state with color — inactive tabs stay --c-faint.
