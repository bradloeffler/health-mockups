FORGE · Kit Components

Screen

The phone-shell primitive. Caps width at 430px, fills the viewport, applies the brand gradient background, and clips anything that would overflow horizontally — the discipline that makes a 430px mock impossible to clip on a 390px device.

Anatomy

Two elements. The outer .k-screen is the shell — fixed-max-width, full-viewport, hidden-overflow, background gradient. The inner .k-screen__scroll is the padded vertical stack that holds the page's content, with built-in safe-area insets.

.k-screen__scroll · the inner column

The padded content stack inside the shell. Vertical flex with gap --s-3 between sections. Pads the safe-area inset top and reserves 96px at the bottom for the floating nav.
