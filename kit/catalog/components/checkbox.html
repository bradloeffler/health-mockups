Checkbox

A 22px square toggle. Unchecked is a hairline outline; checked flips to amber with a centered check glyph. Used in habit rows and any boolean toggle.

Anatomy

A square inline-flex with a 1.5px border, 6px radius, and a check glyph that's transparent in the unchecked state. All three properties, border, bg, glyph color, transition together with --m-fast.

unchecked
checked

States

Two states, two press treatments. Pressed dims briefly to give haptic feedback.

unchecked · default
unchecked · pressed
checked · default
checked · pressed

In context

Checkboxes are most commonly the trailing element on a habit row.
