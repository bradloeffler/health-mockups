Home redesign · fresh perspectives

5 directions

Each is a wildly different take on the same data. Tap any one to feel it on your phone (the Connected ↔ Fresh toggle is on every mock).

A · Editorial calm
Extreme minimal
No card borders anywhere. Long-form rhythm with hairline dividers and a quiet typographic hierarchy.
Boldest move: killed every card container. Spacing does the work that borders used to.
 B · Instrument panel
Bloomberg / WHOOP Pro
A precision dashboard. Sparklines, micro-bars, terminal-tight numbers. Density on purpose.
Boldest move: every biometric shares one matrix. No six-tile grid.
 C · Story panels
Stacked chapters
Each section is a full-width chapter that opens with a giant number or ring beside a sentence. No 2-col grids up top.
Boldest move: 200px recovery ring carries the page like a magazine opener.
 D · Editorial rails
Hero + horizontal rails
One huge hero, then 2-3 snap-scrolling rails of tiles. Spotify / Arc / Robinhood vibe.
Boldest move: reading the day as a one-line verdict ("Primed to push.") instead of a dashboard.
 E · Cockpit
Single-screen, no scroll
The whole Home fits one viewport. Numbers, rings, and tight pills like a watch face for your day.
Boldest move: recovery + strain fused into a single nested ring system.
